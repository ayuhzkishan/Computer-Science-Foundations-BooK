Hello there, future tech expert! \faRocket

Welcome to \textbf{Basic Computer Course (BCC)} --- your personal guide to understanding the amazing world of computers. Whether you've been using computers for years or you're just getting started, this book takes you on an exciting journey from the basics to creating your own mini-projects in plain, simple language.

This book is organised into \textbf{eight modules}. You'll discover what a computer \emph{is}, explore Windows, master keyboard shortcuts, create professional documents in Microsoft Word, crunch numbers in Excel, build stunning presentations in PowerPoint, and navigate the internet safely. 

Every chapter follows a simple pattern: first, we explain the \textbf{theory}, then we provide \textbf{shortcuts and tips}, and finally, \textbf{hands-on lab practicals} to try on a real computer. Keep an eye out for \textbf{Byte} --- our friendly robot mascot who pops up with helpful tips!

One last thing: \textbf{don't worry about getting everything right the first time.} Experiment, make mistakes, and figure out how to fix them. That's exactly how real computer scientists work!
