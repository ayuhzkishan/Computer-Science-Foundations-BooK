\subsection*{Other Symbols You'll See}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=2cm, itemsep=6pt]
  \item[\key{Ctrl}] \textbf{Keyboard keys} are shown as styled key caps like this.
  \item[\textcolor{LabGreen}{\ding{51}}] A \textbf{check mark} means ``Yes'' or ``Correct.''
  \item[\textcolor{WarnRed}{\ding{55}}] A \textbf{cross mark} means ``No'' or ``Incorrect.''
  \item[\faAngleRight] Points in a \textbf{learning objectives} list.
  \item[\faQuestionCircle] Marks a \textbf{review question} at the end of a chapter.
  \item[\stepcircle{1}] A \textbf{step number} in a lab practical.
\end{itemize}

\vspace{10pt}

\begin{notebox}
Every chapter follows this pattern: \textbf{Learn} (theory) $\rightarrow$ \textbf{Practice} (shortcuts \& tips) $\rightarrow$ \textbf{Do} (lab practicals). Work through them in order for the best experience!
\end{notebox}
