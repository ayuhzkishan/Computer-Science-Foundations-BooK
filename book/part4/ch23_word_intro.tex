\begin{notebox}
\textbf{Save} (\key{Ctrl}+\key{S}) saves changes to the current file. \textbf{Save As} (\key{F12}) lets you save a copy with a different name or in a different location. Use Save As when you want to keep the original AND create a modified version.
\end{notebox}

\bytetip[warn]{Get into the habit of pressing \key{Ctrl}+\key{S} every few minutes! There's nothing worse than losing 30 minutes of work because the power went out or the computer froze.}

\begin{funfact}
Microsoft Word was first released in 1983 as ``Multi-Tool Word'' for the Xerox ALTO. Its original price was \textbf{\$375} (about \$1,100 in today's money). Today, you can use Word for free online at office.com!
\end{funfact}
